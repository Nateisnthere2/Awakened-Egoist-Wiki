Overview

Awakened Egoists is an upcoming Roblox sports game centered around soccer/football. Heavily inspired by the popular anime and manga series Blue Lock, the game focuses on egoism, special abilities, and high-stakes strikers' battles.

The game is currently in the development phase, with developers releasing sneak peeks of the UI, maps, and character effects. The official icon features an anime-style male character seen from behind, wearing a dark sports jersey donning the name "ISAGI" and the number "11". The logo features 3D blue text reading "AWAKENED EGOISTS", accompanied by the stylized Japanese text "ブレードッグ" (Bure Doggu).

Auras & Flow State

Taking heavy inspiration from Blue Lock's concept of "Flow" and "Ego", the game features visual aura effects that surround players when they awaken their abilities. Sneak peeks show blocky Roblox avatars surrounded by different colored energy effects representing specific characters and playstyles. Click a card to view details.

Blue Lightning

A crackling blue energy effect. Possibly tied to speed or direct goal-scoring abilities.

Green Flame

A fiery green aura enveloping the character. May represent a defensive or control-type Flow.

Sae Itoshi

A unique pink flame aura surrounded by floating numbers and vines. Represents Sae Itoshi's genius playmaking.
